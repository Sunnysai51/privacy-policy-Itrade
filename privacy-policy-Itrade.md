## 1. Purpose
This policy defines the **public and private usage guidelines** for the Intraday Trading AI Agent.  
The agent is designed to provide **real-time market insights, strategy simulations, and intraday trading recommendations** based on web research, historical analysis, and forecasting.

---

## 2. Public Policy
The **public-facing features** of the AI Agent include:
- **Market Summaries**: General overviews of current market conditions.  
- **Company Insights**: Publicly available information about companies, sectors, and trends.  
- **Trading Strategies**: General intraday strategies (scalping, breakout, momentum, mean reversion).  
- **Risk Alerts**: Public warnings about volatility, macroeconomic events, or sector risks.  
- **Educational Content**: Explanations of trading concepts, technical indicators, and strategy comparisons.  

**Public policy principles:**
- All outputs are **informational only** and not personalized financial advice.  
- The agent uses **publicly available data sources** and does not access private or restricted information without explicit authorization.  
- Transparency: Users are informed when data is simulated, forecasted, or based on historical patterns.
